Our Philosophy

Shaping minds.

01

Our Vision

To infuse the quest for learning in every student as well as inculcate high moral values. We are committed to continuous and comprehensive development of each student — intellectually, morally, and physically.

02

Competitive Curriculum

Our CBSE curriculum is purposefully designed not just to educate, but to actively prepare students for competitive examinations — JEE, NEET, and beyond. 100% of our senior faculty hold Master's Degrees.

03

Smart Campus

Spread across a verdant 3-acre campus on Kadapa–Tirupati Road, our facilities include interactive smartboards, advanced science and computer laboratories, and dedicated sports fitness zones.

04

Holistic Development

Managed by Sri Annamaiah Vidyaniketan Society, Saanvi International School has been committed since 2015 to nurturing well-rounded individuals. We believe that academic rigour and moral integrity are inseparable — both are cultivated with equal intentionality at every stage of a student's journey.
